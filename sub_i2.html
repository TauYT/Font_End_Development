    Langkawi - The Jewel of Kedah

    Discover Langkawi, a breathtaking archipelago of 99 islands in the Andaman Sea. Known as the Jewel of Kedah, Langkawi offers an enticing blend of natural beauty and thrilling adventures. Relax on pristine beaches, explore lush rainforests, or dive into vibrant coral reefs.

    What to Do in Langkawi

    • Sky Bridge Adventure: Take in panoramic views from the iconic Langkawi Sky Bridge.
    • Chill at Pantai Cenang: Unwind on Langkawi's most famous beach.
    • Meet Wildlife: Visit the Langkawi Wildlife Park and get close to diverse species.
    • Dive into History: Explore Mahsuri's Tomb and learn about local legends.

    Travel Tips

    • Stay Sun-Safe: Don't forget sunscreen and a hat.
    • Cash is King: Keep some cash handy for small purchases.
    • Get Around Easily: Rent a car or scooter for convenience.
    • Respect Local Customs: Be mindful of cultural practices at heritage sites.

    Where to Stay

    The Datai Langkawi

    The Datai Langkawi in Teluk Datai offers mountain-view rooms, free bikes, parking, and an outdoor pool. Guests can enjoy a range of dining options, activities like windsurfing and canoeing, and amenities such as a fitness center, garden, and bar. It's a short walk from Datai Bay Beach.

    Four Seasons Resort Langkawi

    Four Seasons Resort Langkawi, located a short walk from Kastam Beach, offers free bikes, private parking, an outdoor pool, and a fitness center. Guests enjoy air-conditioned rooms, a variety of breakfast options, and facilities like a kids' club, tennis courts, and water sports.

    The Andaman, a Luxury Collection Resort

    The Andaman, Langkawi is a luxurious resort on Datai Bay, featuring a private beach, lush rainforest, and elegant rooms with stunning views. It offers top-notch dining, a golf course, water sports, and a spa, all 40 minutes from Langkawi International Airport with free parking and shuttle service.
