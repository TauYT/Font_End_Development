Taman Negara - Malaysia's Ancient Rainforest

Discover Taman Negara, Malaysia's premier national park and one of the world's oldest tropical rainforests. With its rich biodiversity, Taman Negara offers thrilling experiences such as canopy walks, jungle treks, and river cruises. Immerse yourself in the lush greenery and encounter exotic wildlife in this natural paradise.

What to Do in Taman Negara

  • Canopy Walkway: Experience the rainforest from above on the world's longest canopy walkway.
  • Jungle Trekking: Explore the dense forest and discover its diverse flora and fauna.
  • River Cruises: Take a boat ride along the Tembeling and Lata Berkoh rivers.
  • Wildlife Spotting: Look for Malayan tigers, Asian elephants, and exotic birds in their natural habitat.

Travel Tips

  • Prepare for Rain: Bring waterproof clothing and gear as the rainforest can be very wet.
  • Wear Appropriate Footwear: Sturdy hiking boots are recommended for jungle treks.
  • Stay Hydrated: Carry enough water and stay hydrated while exploring.
  • Respect Wildlife: Keep a safe distance from animals and avoid feeding them.

Where to Stay

Mutiara Taman Negara Resort

Mutiara Taman Negara in Kuala Tahan features a garden, terrace, restaurant, and bar. Rooms have air conditioning, flat-screen TV, and private bathrooms. Some offer river views or balconies. Breakfast options include buffet, American, or Asian.

Tebing Guest Houset

TEBiNG Guest House Taman Negara offers riverside accommodations with free WiFi in Kuala Tahan, just 656 feet from the jetty to National Park Malaysia. Some rooms include private bathrooms, and hiking tours are available at an extra cost.

Tekoma Resort, Taman Negara

Tekoma Resort Taman Negara in Kuala Tahan features a garden, outdoor pool, free WiFi, and a restaurant with various cuisine options. An airport shuttle is available.
