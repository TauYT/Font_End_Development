Port Dickson - The Coastal Gem

Explore Port Dickson, a charming coastal town known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant marine life. From relaxing beachside retreats to adventurous water sports, Port Dickson offers something for everyone. Discover the perfect blend of leisure and excitement in this stunning seaside destination.

What to Do in Port Dickson

  • Relax at Teluk Kemang: Enjoy the sun and sea at Port Dickson's most popular beach.
  • Visit the PD Waterfront: Stroll along the promenade and enjoy the seaside atmosphere.
  • Explore Cape Rachado Lighthouse: Climb to the top for panoramic views of the coastline.
  • Experience Water Sports: Try jet skiing, parasailing, or kayaking in the clear waters.

Travel Tips

  • Stay Hydrated: Carry water to stay hydrated under the sun.
  • Plan for Traffic: Port Dickson can get busy, especially on weekends.
  • Local Cuisine: Try local seafood dishes for an authentic experience.
  • Sun Protection: Use sunscreen and wear hats to protect from the sun.

Where to Stay

Grand Hibiscus Port Dickson

Lexis Port Dickson offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, a swimming pool, fitness center, and family-friendly amenities. Located 26 miles from Kuala Lumpur Airport, it features a restaurant, garden, and bike rentals.

Thistle Port Dickson Resort

Thistle Port Dickson, located an hour from Kuala Lumpur Airport, offers 3 km of beach, large pools, a SWAT camp, and scenic views of Tanjung Tuan lighthouse and the Straits of Malacca, perfect for getaways and meetings

Akar Beach Resort

Akar Beach Resort in Port Dickson offers 3-star beachfront accommodations with a garden, shared lounge, and restaurant. Featuring air-conditioned rooms with sea views and free WiFi, it is located 36 miles from Kuala Lumpur Airport and 25 miles from Palm Mall Seremban.
