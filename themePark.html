Escape Park

In Teluk Bahang, Penang, there is an adventure theme park called Escape Adventureplay. ESCAPE has been chosen Malaysia's greatest adventure park on several occasions, thanks to its extensive array of thrilling activities. With its emphasis on zip-lining and other jungle sports, the park has grown to be a popular among those looking for outdoor excitement.

Sunway Lagoon

Located in Petaling Jaya, Kuala Lumpur, is the well-known leisure park Sunway Lagoon Theme Park. It is well-known for housing Asia's first Nickelodeon Theme Park, as well as a water park, scream park, wildlife park, and amusement park.

Lost World of Tambun

The Lost World of Tambun, a theme park and hotel in Sunway City, Ipoh, is well-known for its natural hot springs in addition to its other attractions, which include rides and animals. Surrounded by seven incredible amusement parks, a lush tropical jungle, 400 million-year-old limestone structures, and natural hot springs, this self-contained utopia is truly remarkable.

A Famosa Water Theme Park

Expanding over 20 acres, A'Famosa Water World, Malaysia's largest water theme park, is a popular daytime destination in Melaka. Get ready for some incredible rides, slides, pool activities, and adventure at the A'famosa aquatic world in the heart of Malacca. Escape the chaos of the city. In the southern part of Malaysia, it is the only water park.
