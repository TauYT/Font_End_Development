Cameron Highlands - Discover the Cool Retreat

Explore Cameron Highlands, Malaysia's premier hill station, known for its cool climate and lush landscapes. Nestled in the mountains, Cameron Highlands offers stunning tea plantations, picturesque farms, and vibrant markets. Enjoy a serene retreat and explore the scenic beauty of this highland paradise.

What to Do in Cameron Highlands

  • Visit Tea Plantations: Tour the scenic tea estates and sample fresh tea at Boh Tea Plantation.
  • Explore Mossy Forest: Wander through the mystical moss-covered forest at the highlands' peak.
  • Stroll Through Markets: Shop for fresh produce and local crafts at the bustling night markets.
  • Discover Strawberry Farms: Pick your own strawberries and enjoy delightful strawberry-based treats.

Travel Tips

  • Dress in Layers: The weather can be cool, so wear layers to stay comfortable.
  • Carry Cash: Some local markets and vendors may not accept card payments.
  • Prepare for Rain: Pack a raincoat or umbrella as the highlands can experience sudden showers.
  • Book Accommodations Early: Cameron Highlands is a popular destination, so book your stay in advance.

Where to Stay

Copthorne Hotel Cameron Highlands

Copthorne Cameron Highlands is a 4-star resort in Cameron Highlands featuring a fitness center, garden, terrace, restaurant, indoor pool, and free WiFi. The resort offers room service, a 24-hour front desk, and a playground. Some rooms include a kitchen, and a buffet breakfast is served daily.

The Lakehouse, Cameron Highlands

The Lakehouse Cameron Highlands offers air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities, a garden, and a restaurant. Airport shuttle service is available, with Sultan Azlan Shah Airport 60 miles away.

Cameron Highlands Resort

Nestled amidst stunning landscapes and lush hills, Cameron Highlands Resort features charming Tudor-style cottages and overlooks an 18-hole golf course, making it ideal for exploration and scenic hikes.
