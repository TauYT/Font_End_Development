Pulau Tioman - The Tropical Paradise

Experience the untouched beauty of Pulau Tioman, an idyllic island in the South China Sea. Known for its stunning coral reefs, lush jungles, and pristine beaches, Tioman is a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Enjoy snorkeling, diving, or simply relaxing on the tranquil shores of this tropical gem.

What to Do in Pulau Tioman

  • Snorkeling at Coral Reefs: Discover vibrant marine life and colorful corals.
  • Explore the Rainforest: Hike through lush jungles and discover hidden waterfalls.
  • Relax at Juara Beach: Enjoy serene beach time at one of the island's most beautiful spots.
  • Visit Tioman Island Village: Experience local culture and traditional Malay lifestyle.

Travel Tips

  • Prepare for Outdoor Activities: Bring appropriate gear for hiking and snorkeling.
  • Respect Nature: Follow guidelines to protect the coral reefs and wildlife.
  • Stay Hydrated: Bring plenty of water, especially if you're exploring the island.
  • Local Currency: Ensure you have enough cash, as some places may not accept cards.

Where to Stay

Berjaya Tioman Resort

Berjaya Tioman Resort offers 4-star beachfront accommodations on Tioman Island with an outdoor pool, fitness center, and garden. It features air-conditioned rooms, a restaurant, free WiFi, and a kids' club. ABC Beach Jetty is 2.8 miles away.

Paya Beach Resort

Paya Beach Spa & Dive Resort is a 4-star beachfront resort on Tioman Island with white sand beaches, coral reefs, and a range of packages including diving, spa treatments, and honeymoon options.

Japamala Resort

Japamala Resort by Samadhi on Tioman Island offers an outdoor pool, garden, bar, and free WiFi. Rooms include balconies, private bathrooms, and flat-screen TVs. The resort serves varied breakfast options and has a restaurant. Snorkeling is available.
